Action=TogglePowerÂ¶

In Tiberian Sun there was a button on the sidebar that allowed players to turn their own buildings on and off and in low power situations. In Yuri’s Revenge, a super weapon with Action=TogglePower achieves the same function. However, if such a super weapon were fired into an empty cell then an Internal Error would occur. Ares prevents the error occurring.

New in version 0.1.
