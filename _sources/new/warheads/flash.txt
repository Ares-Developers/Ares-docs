 Warheads; Flashing effect

Flashing


When a unit is hit by this warhead, it can be made to flash. This effect is used
to indicate a unit being repaired or healed (7 frames) or promoted. In
Red Alert, flashing was also used when a unit was hit by a
Charges=yes weapon (4 frames).

Whether this effect is applied when no conventional damage is dealt can be
customized. See ....

[Warhead]Flash.Duration=integer - frames
  If positive, the victim hit by this warhead will flash for the specified
  amount of frames. Defaults to 0.
