Facings on Units


Ares supports to have more than just 8 facings for VehicleTypes.
Set in artmd.ini:

[VehicleType]Facings=integer
  The number of facings used for SHP based units. Supports powers of 2 equal to
  or larger than 8. Values less than 8 result in only the
  first frame being used. Defaults to 8.
