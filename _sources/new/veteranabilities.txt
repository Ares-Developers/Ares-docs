Veteran and Elite Abilities


This is a list of all new veteran and elite abilities Ares supports.

+ EMPIMMUNE
  See Electromagnetic Pulse Immunity.

+ RADIMMUNE
  Makes the unit immune to radiation like ImmuneToRadiation. This affects
  direct hits from Radiation=yes warheads as well as from radiation sites
  spread by warheads with RadLevel greater than 0.

+ PROTECTED_DRIVER
  See Killing Drivers and Protected Drivers.

+ UNWARPABLE
  Makes units immune to Temporal=yes weapons like Warpable=no.

+ POISONIMMUNE
  Makes units immune to Poison=yes weapons like
  ImmuneToPoison=yes.

+ PSIONICWEAPONIMMUNE
  Makes units immune to PsychicDamage=yes weapons like
  ImmuneToPsionicWeapons=yes.

+ PSIONICSIMMUNE
  Makes units immune to Psychedelic=yes and MindControl=yes
  weapons like ImmuneToPsionics=yes. The Psychic Dominator can still
  override this effect just like the unconditional ImmuneToPsionics=yes.
