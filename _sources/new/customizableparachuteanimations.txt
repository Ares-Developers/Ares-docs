Customizable Parachute Animations


Units can now have their own individual parachute animation.

[TechnoType]Parachute.Anim=animation
  Specifies the animation from artmd.ini to use as the unit's parachute
  when it is falling. If omitted, Parachute.Anim will default to
  PARACH. The SHP is expected to be in the unit palette.
