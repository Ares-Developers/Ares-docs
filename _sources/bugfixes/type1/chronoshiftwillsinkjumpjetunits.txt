Chronoshift Will Sink Jumpjet Units


If a jumpjet unit with SpeedType=Hover like the Nighthawk or the deployed
Siege Chopper was chronoshifted into water it originally would not sink. The
unit would even be able to take off unharmed. Ares will let these
jumpjet units sink.
