Spying on Allied BuildingsÂ¶

In Yuri’s Revenge players are not supposed to infiltrate own or allied buildings, yet it was possible to order a spy to do so. When a spy entered a building that was owned by a player allied to the spy owner, the spy effects would still apply. This could be exploited easily. Ares prevents this.

New in version 0.4.
