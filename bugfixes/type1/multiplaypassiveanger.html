MultiplayPassive=yes Houses Anger PlayersÂ¶

AI players pick their favorite enemy by detecting which enemy player caused the most damage and destruction. If the neutral houses caused some damage near the start of the match (which could also happen if Oil Derricks blew up), AI players might pick a neutral house as their enemy to go after and thus start a war against civilians.

With Ares, MultiplayPassive=yes countries are no longer eligible to be picked as favorite enemy.

New in version 3.0.
