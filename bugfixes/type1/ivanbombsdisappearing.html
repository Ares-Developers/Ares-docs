Disappearing Ivan BombsÂ¶

Ivan Bombs attached to a unit just vanished when the unit deployed into a building and vice versa. The bomb would also disappear if a rigged building changed its owner. If a building was sold while a bomb was attached to it, the bomb was sold along with it, without being triggered.

Ares transfers the bomb in such cases and prevents the bomb from getting lost in the process. If a unit or building is sold, the bomb will now go off.

New in version 0.2.
