Temporal Warheads Earn Experience By Killing Friendly UnitsÂ¶

When a unit kills an enemy unit it gains experience, which can lead to that unit becoming veteran or elite. Killing friendly units (your own or your allies’) does not earn experience in this way.

However, Temporal warheads (warheads with Temporal=yes set) did use to earn experience when killing friendly units. This is no longer the case.

New in version 0.1.
