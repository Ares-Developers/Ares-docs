Cloaked Objects no Longer Uncloak if UnaffectedÂ¶

Cloaked objects being hit by warheads that dealt no damage to them or even healed them uncloaked briefly. It was unintuitive for friendly warheads to reveal units and structures to the enemy. Ares changed this behavior so healing weapons and weapons dealing no damage will no longer uncloak objects.

New in version 0.E.
