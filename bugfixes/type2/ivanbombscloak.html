Ivan Bombs and Cloaked IvansÂ¶

In Yuri’s Revenge, a unit delivering damage is immune to this damage, unless stated otherwise by DamageSelf=yes. If an Ivan cloaked after placing a bomb, the bomb ‘forgot’ who planted it. Thus, the Ivan was not counted as the source of the damage once the bomb exploded, and thus it could take damage from it. Ares prevents this from happening, and cloaking Ivans are now as immune to their own bombs as non-cloaking Ivans are.

New in version 0.6.
