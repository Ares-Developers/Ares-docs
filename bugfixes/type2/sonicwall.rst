=========================================
Sonic Waves Respect :captiontag:`Wall=no`
=========================================

:game:`Yuri's Revenge` hardcoded sonic waves to always destroy wall section
overlay. With :game:`Ares` they will only do so if the warhead has
:tag:`Wall=yes` set.

.. versionadded:: 3.0
