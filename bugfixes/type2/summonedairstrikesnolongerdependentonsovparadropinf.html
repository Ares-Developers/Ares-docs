Summoned Airstrikes no longer dependent on SovParaDropInf¶

If a you were to remove the [General]►SovParaDropInf tag, then summoned Airstrikes such as Boris’ weapon will trigger an Internal Error when fired. This no longer happens in Ares.

New in version 0.2.
