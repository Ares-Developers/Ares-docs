DigSound¶

[AudioVisual]►DigSound is used for both the global Nuke siren and the sound made when a subterranean unit (with Locomotor={4A582743-9839-11d1-B709-00A024DDAFD1}) digs into or emerges from the ground. With Ares, the super weapon can specify its own SW.ActivationSound (which defaults to [AudioVisual]►DigSound for nukes).

New in version 0.1.
