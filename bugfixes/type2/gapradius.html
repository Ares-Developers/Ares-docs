Gap Generator Radius can be greater than 127 CellsÂ¶

Due to Yuri’s Revenge using a too small data type, the radius for the Gap Generators could not be greater than 127 cells. Ares reimplemented the tags and now GapRadiusInCells= and SuperGapRadiusInCells= support greater values.

Note

Use this new freedom with caution. Including too many cells can cause lag.

New in version 3.0.
