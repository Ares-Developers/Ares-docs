Initial Veteran CameosÂ¶

When you infiltrate, say, an enemy Barracks, any (trainable) infantry that you subsequently build will start veteran. The infantry’s cameos (build icons) will gain the veteran symbol to indicate this. However, if the country you are playing as has any units that always ‘start veteran’ then those units did not gain the veteran symbol for their cameos. Now they do.

New in version 0.1.
