Occupiers are always trained even if Trainable=noÂ¶

Units occupying civilian structures or Battle Bunkers unconditionally gain experience from kills even if Trainable=no is set. In the original game, GIs, Conscripts and Initiates are all Trainable=yes, and thus the problem does not arise. Ares fixes this.
