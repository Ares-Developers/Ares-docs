Play Sound at Random WaypointÂ¶

The map action Play Sound at Random Waypoint collected up to 400 waypoints from which it then randomly selected one to play a sound effect at. This could crash, because it had actually only space to store 100 waypoints. If there were more waypoints present on the map, the game would crash. This has been fixed.

New in version 0.5.
