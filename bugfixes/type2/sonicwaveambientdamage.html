Sonic Wave Ambient Damage¶

The Warhead and AmbientDamage settings used when applying ambient damage from a sonic weapon were always taken from the unit’s current primary weapon rather than the weapon that actually fired the wave. Now these settings are taken from the weapon that fired the wave.

New in version 0.1.
