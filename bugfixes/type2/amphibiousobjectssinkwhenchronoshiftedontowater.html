Amphibious Objects Sink When Chronoshifted Onto WaterÂ¶

When an amphibious (not hover) object - that is, one that can normally enter water cells without problem - was chronoshifted onto water by the Chronosphere, it would sink. Ares corrects this so that all amphibious objects can be chronoshifted onto water without sinking.

New in version 0.2.
