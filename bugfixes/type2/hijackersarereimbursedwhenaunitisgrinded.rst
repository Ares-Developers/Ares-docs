Hijackers are reimbursed when a unit is grinded
```````````````````````````````````````````````

If you send a hijacked unit into a grinder, you will get a refund for
the vehicle thief also. Originally, Yuri's Revenge did not consider
the hijacker.

.. versionadded:: 0.2
