Repair Wrench Revealing Cloaked StructuresÂ¶

The wrench animation appearing when a building is being repaired could give away building positions to enemies as it was visible for everyone even if the structure was cloaked. This has been fixed and buildings will not show the repair animation while cloaked. Observers will still see the wrench, though.

New in version 0.8.
