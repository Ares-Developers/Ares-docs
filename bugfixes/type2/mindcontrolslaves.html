Slaves Change Ownership If Enslaver Switches SidesÂ¶

In Yuri’s Revenge the Slave Miner was the only unit that enslaved InfantryTypes, and it was immune to mind-control and couldn’t be captured. If it could be captured however, you would notice the slaves wouldn’t change their ownership along with the vehicle - they would stay on the original owner’s side.

In Ares, slaves will also change their ownership if their enslaving unit is captured or mind-controlled.

New in version 0.2.
