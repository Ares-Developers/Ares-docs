Force Shield¶

Like the Iron Curtain the Force Shield duration can be customized for each BuildingType.

[BuildingType]►ForceShield.Modifier= (float - multiplier)

The number of seconds of Force Shield duration is multiplied by this value. Valid values are 0% (Force Shield disallowed) or higher. Defaults to 100%.

Note

Buildings with a ForceShield.Modifier value of 0.0 or less are not eligible to fire a Force Shield super weapon on.

New in version 0.6.

Changed in version 0.8.
