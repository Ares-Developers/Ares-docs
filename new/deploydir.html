DeployDir¶

Originally, this setting was used for DeployToLand=yes units. When ordered to deploy, they would turn towards this direction first.

With Ares, all units having a DeployingAnim will turn towards this direction first when deploying, ignoring DeployToLand. This way, deploying anims can hide the transition of unit types better, while units not having a DeployingAnim do not turn needlessly.

[TechnoType]►DeployDir= (integer - facing)
The direction a unit will face when deploying. Valid values range from 0 for north to 7 for north west. Defaults to [General]►DeployDir.

New in version 2.0.
