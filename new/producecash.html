Oil Derricks (ProduceCash)Â¶

Building upgrades now support ProduceCashDelay, ProduceCashAmount and ProduceCashStartup like normal buildings. Each upgrade manages its own timer, so they will grant the amount independently of the main building or other upgrades.

ProduceCashStartup has been expanded to support negative values. The amount is withdrawn from the capturing player’s account. If less than the specified amount is available, then the building can still be captured or placed.

New in version 0.9.
